STRE Perpetual Preferred Stock

As previously disclosed, on November 13, 2025, Strategy completed its previously announced public offering of 7,750,000 shares of its 10.00% Series A Perpetual Stream Preferred Stock (the “STRE Offering”) at a public offering price of €80.00 per share. The gross proceeds from the STRE Offering were approximately €620.0 million (or $716.8 million) and the net proceeds from the STRE Offering were approximately €608.8 million (or $703.9 million), after deducting the underwriting discounts and commissions and Strategy's estimated offering expense. Strategy’s gross proceeds and net proceeds in U.S. dollars disclosed herein are calculated based upon the Euro to U.S. dollar exchange rate of €1.00/$1.1561.

Item 7.01 Regulation FD Disclosure.

 

Strategy Dashboard

Strategy also maintains a dashboard on its website (www.strategy.com) as a disclosure channel for providing broad, non-exclusionary distribution of information regarding Strategy to the public, including information regarding market prices of its outstanding securities, bitcoin purchases and holdings, certain key performance indicator metrics and other supplemental information, and as one means of disclosing non-public information in compliance with its disclosure obligations under Regulation FD. Investors and others are encouraged to regularly review the information that Strategy makes public via the website dashboard.
