USD Reserve Update

Strategy has maintained a U.S. dollar reserve (the "USD Reserve") to support the payment of dividends on its preferred stock and interest on its outstanding indebtedness.

As of January 4, 2026, the balance of the USD Reserve was $2.25 billion.

The maintenance of the USD Reserve, as well as its terms and amount, remain subject to Strategy’s sole and absolute discretion. Strategy may adjust the USD Reserve from time-to-time based on market conditions, liquidity needs and other factors.
