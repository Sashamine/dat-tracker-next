<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><b>Item 1.01. Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement.</b></p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><b>&#160;</b></p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-indent: 0.5in">On October 27, 2025, Hyperion DeFi, Inc. (&#8220;Hyperion&#8221;
or the &#8220;Company&#8221;) entered into a Joint Validator Operators&#8217; Agreement (the &#8220;Joint Validator Agreement&#8221;)
with Kinetiq Research Pte. Ltd. (&#8220;Kinetiq Group&#8221;) and Pier Two Pty Ltd (&#8220;Pier Two&#8221;), effective retroactively to
June 25, 2025. The Joint Validator Agreement formalizes the parties&#8217; collaboration in jointly operating a co-branded validator node
(&#8220;Kinetiq &#215; Hyperion&#8221; or &#8220;KxH Node&#8221;) on the Hyperliquid Layer-1 blockchain (&#8220;Hyperliquid&#8221;).</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-indent: 0.5in">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-indent: 0.5in">Under the Joint Validator Agreement, Hyperion&#160;initiated
the validator with 10,000 HYPE and agreed to provide&#160;staking capital from its treasury of HYPE tokens,&#160;so that the validator
enters Hyperliquid&#8217;s active set of validators and it is eligible to produce and attest blocks in the Hyperliquid consensus protocol.
Kinetiq Group&#160;will contribute validator operations support, smart contract infrastructure, and stake-routing tooling via its liquid&#160;staking
protocols, and Pier Two&#160;will host and manage the validator infrastructure, including uptime, monitoring and security, and will maintain
ISO/IEC 27001 and SOC 2 compliance.</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-indent: 0.5in">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-indent: 0.5in">The Joint Validator Agreement outlines shared responsibilities
for validator operations, governance, incident response, and performance monitoring. It includes a revenue-sharing arrangement whereby
staking commissions and other validator-level rewards are allocated among Hyperion, Kinetiq Group and Pier Two, with specific overrides
for referred delegations. The Joint Validator Agreement also includes provisions for key management and quorum-based control of validator
cryptographic material; service level obligations and remedies for performance shortfalls; and risk management and indemnification for
slashing events or operational failures.</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-indent: 0.5in">The Joint Validator Agreement is effective for
an initial term of one year and will automatically renew annually unless terminated by any party with 90 days&#8217; notice. The agreement
may also be terminated by any party for an event of default after a cure period; by any non-affected party if a force majeure event continues
for more than a specified number of consecutive calendar days and materially impairs another party&#8217;s ability to carry out its obligations
under the Joint Validator Agreement; and by Hyperion, at any time in its sole discretion, if the KxH Node remains unable to meet a specified
active stake or falls out of the active set.</p>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: center"><B>Hyperion DeFi Announces Strategic Partnership

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: center"><I>&nbsp;</I></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: center"><I>New Hyperliquid Product Compounds Yield on 500,000
HYPE Staked by the Company </I></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">LAGUNA HILLS, Calif., October 29, 2025 -- Hyperion DeFi, Inc. (NASDAQ:
HYPD) (&ldquo;Hyperion DeFi&rdquo; or the &ldquo;Company&rdquo;), the first U.S. publicly listed company dedicated to building a long-term
strategic treasury of Hyperliquid&rsquo;s native token, HYPE, today announced a strategic partnership with HyperEVM protocol Felix (&ldquo;Felix&rdquo;),
marking a significant milestone in decentralized finance innovation.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">Under the newly signed HYPE Asset Use Service (HAUS) agreement, Hyperion
DeFi will provide the use of 500,000 HYPE to Felix, supporting the protocol&rsquo;s ability to launch and manage a custom on-chain perpetual
futures market using Hyperliquid&rsquo;s revolutionary HIP-3 framework. HIP-3 (Hyperliquid Improvement Proposal 3) enables the permissionless
creation of markets for non-crypto assets &ndash; including equities, commodities, and indices, ushering in a new era of decentralized
trading.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">Approved users will gain access to trade Felix-deployed HIP-3 markets,
and trading fees will be distributed between the Hyperliquid protocol, Felix and Hyperion DeFi. This initiative not only expands Hyperliquid&rsquo;s
product suite and potential user base but also unlocks new revenue streams for Hyperion DeFi&rsquo;s staked HYPE assets.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&ldquo;HAUS Felix is a testament to Hyperion DeFi&rsquo;s leadership
in simultaneously pioneering novel financial primitives developed on Hyperliquid, and amplifying the value of our HYPE holdings,&rdquo;
said Hyunsu Jung, Interim Chief Executive Officer of Hyperion DeFi. &ldquo;We&rsquo;re excited to partner with Felix to push the boundaries
of DeFi innovation, powered by Hyperliquid.&rdquo;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">Felix, one of the earliest DeFi protocols built on HyperEVM, crossed
$1 billion TVL (total value locked) in September 2025. Its suite of borrow/lend products &ndash; including Felix CDP and Felix Vanilla
 &ndash; has helped scale liquidity access and stablecoin minting for users on Hyperliquid. In its next phase, Felix plans to integrate
its DeFi infrastructure in the buildout of Felix Exchange.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&ldquo;Since inception, our team has had the vision to bring Felix
to the scale of traditional markets, building bridges for different forms of Hyperliquid adoption across markets,&rdquo; added Charlie
Ambrose, Co-Founder of Felix. &ldquo;We&rsquo;re confident this partnership with Hyperion DeFi will accelerate the scaling of Felix and
Hyperliquid&rsquo;s total addressable market by magnitudes.&rdquo;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">This launch is among the first to utilize Hyperliquid&rsquo;s HIP-3

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><B>About the Hyperliquid Platform and the HYPE Token</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">Hyperliquid is a next-generation layer one blockchain optimized for
high frequency, transparent finance. The blockchain includes fully on-chain perpetual futures and spot order books, with every order,
cancel, trade, and liquidation occurring within 70 millisecond block times. It also hosts the HyperEVM, a general-purpose smart contract
platform that supports permissionless decentralized financial applications akin to Ethereum.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">HYPE is the native token of Hyperliquid. Staked HYPE provides utility
for users via reduced trading fees and enhanced referral bonuses. As of October 2025, over 30 million HYPE has been autonomously purchased
and sequestered by the blockchain with the trading fees generated on the network&rsquo;s order books.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

fastest growing, highest revenue-generating blockchains in the world. Shareholders benefit from compounding exposure to HYPE, both from
its native staking yield and additional revenues generated from its unique on-chain utility.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>
