<P STYLE="font: 14pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: center"><FONT STYLE="font-size: 10pt"><B>TAO Synergies
Acquires $10 Million of Bittensor (TAO) to Become<BR>
 Largest Publicly Traded Pure Play TAO Holder</B></FONT></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: center"><B>&nbsp;</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: center"><B>29,899 TAO tokens purchased to date</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: center"><B>&nbsp;</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: center"><B>Stakes tokens in decentralized AI network for
yield generation</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: center">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">NEW YORK, July 17, 2025 &ndash; TAO Synergies Inc. (Nasdaq: TAOX) (the
 &ldquo;Company&rdquo;), a digital asset treasury company focused on Bittensor (TAO), the premier crypto token for decentralized artificial
intelligence (AI), today announced that as of July 17, 2025, it has acquired 29,899 TAO tokens for a total purchase price of $10.0 million,
at an average price of approximately $334 per token. The purchases of TAO are in accordance with the Company&rsquo;s digital asset treasury
strategy directed toward TAO, the native token for Bittensor, a decentralized AI network that incentivizes the development of machine
intelligence.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">TAO Synergies is now the largest publicly traded pure play TAO holder
worldwide.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&ldquo;We&rsquo;re pleased with how we&rsquo;ve positioned our initial
$10 million in TAO purchases, having established our holdings at what we believe to be an attractive entry point relative to current market
levels. We will continue to purchase TAO tokens as part of our ongoing strategy,&rdquo; said Executive Chairman Joshua Silverman. &ldquo;Our
conviction in TAO is driven by the expected continued growth and expansion of decentralized AI, which is gaining significant traction
as a portion of total AI spend. We believe that decentralized AI has the potential to eventually surpass centralized AI.&rdquo;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&ldquo;Decentralized AI and TAO reminds me of the Internet in 1996
or Bitcoin in 2013/2014. Decentralized AI is still in early days, but accelerated growth is expected as adopters seek the strong near-
and long-term benefits,&rdquo; said James Altucher, digital asset treasury strategist for TAO Synergies. &ldquo;Spending on decentralized
AI, and TAO in particular, is rising rapidly&nbsp;as investors seek exposure to the next phase of AI infrastructure growth.&rdquo;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">Traditional AI is dominated by centralized tech giants and expensive
proprietary models, while access to GPUs is becoming increasingly costly and constrained as AI systems grow more complex. In contrast,
decentralized AI networks offer scalable, open participation through token-based incentive, drawing a growing share of AI investment capital.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">TAO Synergies is committed to providing transparent updates on treasury
holdings and yields periodically in press releases, events and 10-Q and 10-K filings with the SEC.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><B>About TAO Synergies</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><B>&nbsp;</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">TAO Synergies Inc. is the first pure-play public company focused on
the convergence between&nbsp;cryptocurrency&nbsp;and artificial intelligence.&nbsp;The Company&rsquo;s differentiated&nbsp;cryptocurrency&nbsp;treasury
strategy is centered exclusively on the acquisition of TAO, the native cryptocurrency of&nbsp;Bittensor, a&nbsp;decentralized&nbsp;blockchain
network for machine learning and AI. TAO Synergies Inc. seeks to stake TAO for revenue generation and capital appreciation, a strategy
that underscores its mission to create significant value for shareholders.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">Further information is available at <U>taosynergies.com</U>.</P>
