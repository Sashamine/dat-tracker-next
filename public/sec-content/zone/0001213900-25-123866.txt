<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt"> <b>Item 5.07</b> <b>Submission of Matters to a Vote of Security Holders.</b></p>



<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify; background-color: white">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify; background-color: white"><span style="background-color: white">On
December 17, 2025, CleanCore Solutions, Inc. (the &#8220;Company&#8221;) held its 2025 annual meeting of stockholders (the &#8220;<span style="text-decoration: underline">Annual
Meeting</span>&#8221;). At the Annual Meeting, a total of 73,585,935 shares of the Company&#8217;s common stock were represented in person
