<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: center"><B>Twenty One to Begin Trading on NYSE Under Ticker
&ldquo;XXI&rdquo; with over 43,500 Bitcoin in Holdings after Successful Completion of Business Combination</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: center"><I>&nbsp;</I></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: center"><I>Bitcoin-native public company, co-founded by
Tether and Jack Mallers and backed by Bitfinex and SoftBank Group, completes business combination with Cantor Equity Partners</I></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">AUSTIN, Texas -- Twenty One Capital, Inc. (&ldquo;Twenty
One&rdquo;), a Bitcoin-native company, today announced the completion of its business combination (the &ldquo;Business Combination&rdquo;)
with Cantor Equity Partners, Inc. (&ldquo;CEP&rdquo;). Twenty One&rsquo;s Class A common stock will begin trading on the New York Stock
Exchange (&ldquo;NYSE&rdquo;) on December 9, 2025, under the ticker &ldquo;XXI.&rdquo;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">Twenty One is majority-owned by Tether Investments,
S.A. de C.V. (&ldquo;Tether&rdquo;), the world&rsquo;s largest stablecoin issuer, and Bitfinex, with significant minority ownership by
SoftBank Group Corp. (&ldquo;SoftBank Group&rdquo;), one of the world&rsquo;s leading investment holding companies.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">Twenty One holds over 43,500 Bitcoin, making it
the world&rsquo;s third largest public corporate holder of Bitcoin. Twenty One pairs this treasury with an operating business focused
on building out Bitcoin-native financial services, capital markets advisory, lending, and educational media designed to accelerate Bitcoin&rsquo;s
integration into the global financial system.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">&ldquo;Bitcoin is honest money. That's why people
choose it, and that's why we built Twenty One on top of it.&rdquo; said Jack Mallers, Co-Founder and CEO of Twenty One. &ldquo;Listing
on the NYSE is about giving Bitcoin the place it deserves in global markets and giving investors the best of Bitcoin: its strength as
a reserve and the upside of a business built on it.&rdquo;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">Twenty One has committed to operating with public-market
transparency from day one, including publishing on-chain proof of its Bitcoin holdings at https://xxi.mempool.space to allow real-time
verification by shareholders. Twenty One is a Bitcoin-native company that will strategically allocate capital to increase Bitcoin per
share. Twenty One intends to develop a corporate architecture capable of supporting financial products built with and on Bitcoin. This
includes native lending models, capital market instruments, and future innovations that will replace legacy financial tools with Bitcoin-aligned
alternatives. This approach is designed to give public-market investors exposure to disciplined Bitcoin accumulation within a purpose-built
operating structure.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">Alongside its accumulation strategy, Twenty One
plans to build a set of Bitcoin-centric operating businesses intended to generate recurring revenue and expand institutional engagement
with Bitcoin. Efforts will focus on educational content and branded media to support Bitcoin literacy, as well as the rollout of Bitcoin-aligned
alternatives to legacy financial services offerings. These business lines would create complementary income streams that reinforce Twenty
One&rsquo;s Bitcoin strategy and broaden the market infrastructure built around the asset.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">&nbsp;</P>
