<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify"><B>Insider Letter Amendment</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify"><FONT STYLE="background-color: white">As previously
disclosed, on April 22, 2025, contemporaneously with the execution of </FONT>the business combination agreement, dated as of April 22,
2025 (as amended on July 26, 2025) by and among Cantor Equity Partners, Inc., a Cayman Islands exempted company (&ldquo;CEP&rdquo;), Twenty
One Capital, Inc., a Texas corporation (the &ldquo;Company&rdquo;), Twenty One Merger Sub D, a Cayman Islands exempted company and wholly owned subsidiary
of the Company, Twenty One Assets, LLC, a Delaware limited liability company, Tether Investments, S.A. de C.V., an El Salvador&nbsp;<I>sociedad
an&oacute;nima de capital variable</I>, iFinex, Inc., a British Virgin Islands company and, solely for certain limited purposes, Stellar
Beacon LLC, a Delaware limited liability company (the &ldquo;Business Combination Agreement&rdquo;)<FONT STYLE="background-color: white">,
the Company, CEP and Cantor EP Holdings, LLC (the &ldquo;Sponsor&rdquo;) entered into the Sponsor Support Agreement (the &ldquo;Sponsor
Support Agreement&rdquo;), where, among other things, the parties agreed to permit CEP and the Sponsor to amend, prior to Closing (as
defined in the Business Combination Agreement), the letter agreement, dated as of August 12, 2024 by and among CEP, the Sponsor and the
then current directors and executive officers of CEP (the &ldquo;Insider Letter&rdquo;), to shorten the lock-up&nbsp;period applicable
to the Founder Shares (as defined in the Insider Letter) from one (1)&nbsp;year to six (6)&nbsp;months. </FONT></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify"><FONT STYLE="background-color: white">On December
5, 2025, pursuant to the Sponsor Support Agreement, CEP, the Sponsor and the Company entered into an amendment to the Insider Letter (the
&ldquo;Insider Letter Amendment&rdquo;), pursuant to which the Insider Letter was amended to (a) add the Company as a party to the Insider Letter; (b) revise the terms of the Insider Letter
to include Class&nbsp;A common stock of the Company, par value $0.01 per share, which pursuant to the Business Combination Agreement is
issued in exchange for the CEP&rsquo;s Class A ordinary shares; and (c) shorten the lock-up&nbsp;period applicable to the Founder
Shares from one (1)&nbsp;year to six (6)&nbsp;months and amend the terms of the lock-up set forth in Section 7 of the Insider Letter to
reflect the terms of the lock-up agreements to be entered into pursuant to the Business Combination Agreement, at Closing.</FONT></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">As previously disclosed, on December 8, 2025,
the Company consummated the transactions contemplated by the Business Combination Agreement.</P>
