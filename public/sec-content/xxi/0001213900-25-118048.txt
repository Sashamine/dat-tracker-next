Expected Closing of Business Combination and NYSE Listing</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: center"><B>&nbsp;</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 0; text-align: center"><B>Expected Closing December 8, 2025; Combined Company
Expected to Begin Trading on NYSE Under Ticker &ldquo;XXI&rdquo; on December 9, 2025</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: center">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; text-align: justify; margin: 0">December 3, 2025</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; text-align: justify; margin: 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; text-align: justify; margin: 0">AUSTIN, Texas--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Twenty One Capital, Inc. (&ldquo;Twenty
One&rdquo;), the first-ever Bitcoin-native company that expects to be publicly listed, and Cantor Equity Partners, Inc. (&ldquo;CEP&rdquo;)
(Nasdaq: CEP) today announced that, at the Extraordinary General Meeting of CEP&rsquo;s shareholders (the &ldquo;Meeting&rdquo;) held
today, CEP&rsquo;s shareholders approved the previously announced proposed business combination between the parties (the &ldquo;Business
Combination&rdquo;), as well as all other proposals related to the Business Combination.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; text-align: justify; margin: 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; text-align: justify; margin: 0">The final voting results for the Meeting will be included in a Current
Report on Form 8-K to be filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission (the &ldquo;SEC&rdquo;) by CEP.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; text-align: justify; margin: 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; text-align: justify; margin: 0">Based on today&rsquo;s shareholder approval and subject to the satisfaction
or waiver of certain other closing conditions as described in CEP&rsquo;s definitive proxy statement and Twenty One&rsquo;s final prospectus,
the Business Combination and related PIPE financings are expected to be consummated on or about December 8, 2025. Following the consummation
of such transactions, the combined company will operate as Twenty One Capital, Inc. and its shares of Class A common stock are expected
to trade on the New York Stock Exchange (&ldquo;NYSE&rdquo;) beginning on December 9, 2025 under the symbol &ldquo;XXI.&rdquo;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; text-align: justify; margin: 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; text-align: justify; margin: 0"><B>About Twenty One Capital, Inc.</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; text-align: justify; margin: 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; text-align: justify; margin: 0">At closing, Twenty One will be a newly formed operating company focused
exclusively on Bitcoin-related business lines that, among other things, offer shareholders a differentiated opportunity to gain exposure
to Bitcoin through the equity markets. With a Bitcoin-native operating structure and a strategy designed to deliver long-term value, Twenty
One intends to become a leading vehicle for capital-efficient Bitcoin accumulation and related business development.</P>
