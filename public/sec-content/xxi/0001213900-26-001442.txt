<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify"><b><i>Modifications to CFO Option Award Agreement</i></b></p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">As previously disclosed, on December 8, 2025,
Steven Meehan and Twenty One Capital, Inc. (the &#8220;Company&#8221;) entered into an employment agreement (the &#8220;CFO Employment
Agreement&#8221;), setting forth the terms of Mr. Meehan&#8217;s employment, including his compensation, which is described in, and filed
with, the Company&#8217;s Current Report on Form 8-K filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission on December 12, 2025.</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">Pursuant to the CFO Employment Agreement, on December
8, 2025, Mr. Meehan and the Company entered into an option award agreement, where Mr. Meehan received an award (the &#8220;CFO Initial
Award&#8221;) of stock options to purchase 941,620 shares of &#160;Class A common stock of the Company, par value $0.01 per share (&#8220;Class
A Common Stock&#8221;), with an exercise price of $14.43 per share (the &#8220;Prior Option Award Agreement&#8221;), which is described
in the Company&#8217;s Current Report on Form 8-K filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission on December 12, 2025.</p>
