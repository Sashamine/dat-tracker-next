<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">On May 20, 2025, KULR Technology
Group, Inc. (the &#8220;Company&#8221; or &#8220;KULR&#8221;) issued a press release announcing that the Company acquired additional bitcoin
of approximately $9 million, at a weighted average price of $103,234 per bitcoin, inclusive of fees and expenses. KULR also announced
that its year-to-date BTC Yield, a key performance indicator described below, was 220.2%.</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify"><b>BTC Yield as a Key Performance Indicator (KPI)</b></p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">KULR uses BTC Yield as a KPI
to help assess the performance of its strategy of acquiring bitcoin in a manner KULR believes is accretive to stockholders. KULR believes
this KPI can be used to supplement an investor&#8217;s understanding of KULR&#8217;s decision to fund the acquisition of bitcoin by issuing
additional shares of its common stock. As further described below, when KULR uses this KPI, management also takes into account the various
limitations of this metric.</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify"><b>Important Information about BTC Yield KPI</b></p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">BTC Yield is a KPI that represents
the percentage change period-to-period of the ratio between KULR&#8217;s bitcoin holdings and its assumed diluted shares outstanding.
Assumed diluted shares outstanding refers to the aggregate of KULR&#8217;s actual shares of common stock outstanding as of the end of
each period plus all additional shares that would result from the settlement of and exercise of all outstanding derivative securities.
Assumed diluted shares outstanding is not calculated using the treasury method and does not take into account any vesting conditions or
the exercise price of any derivative securities.</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">Additionally, BTC Yield is
not, and should not be understood as, an operating performance measure, or a financial or liquidity measure. In particular, BTC Yield
is not equivalent to a &#8220;yield&#8221; in the traditional financial context. It is not a measure of the return on investment KULR&#8217;s
stockholders may have achieved historically or can achieve in the future by purchasing stock of KULR, or a measure of income generated
by KULR&#8217;s operations or its bitcoin holdings, return on investment on its bitcoin holdings, or any other similar financial measure
of the performance of its business or assets.</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">The trading price of KULR&#8217;s
common stock is impacted by numerous factors in addition to the amount of bitcoins KULR holds and number of actual or potential shares
of its stock outstanding. As a result, the market value of KULR&#8217;s shares may trade at a discount or a premium relative to the market
value of the bitcoin KULR holds. BTC Yield is not indicative nor predictive of the trading price of KULR&#8217;s shares of common stock.
As noted above, this KPI is narrow in its purpose and is used by management to assist in assessing whether KULR is using equity capital
in a manner accretive to stockholders solely as it pertains to its bitcoin holdings.</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">In calculating this KPI, KULR
does not take into account the source of capital used for the acquisition of its bitcoin. KULR notes in particular, it could acquire bitcoin
using cash flow from operations, if any, as well as proceeds from external financings including, but not limited to, from the sale of
shares in its at-the-market (ATM) offering. Accordingly, this metric might overstate or understate the accretive nature of KULR&#8217;s
use of equity capital to acquire bitcoin because not all bitcoin may be acquired using proceeds of equity offerings and not all issuances
of equity may involve the acquisition of bitcoin.</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">KULR&#8217;s ability to achieve
positive BTC Yield may depend on a variety of factors, including, but not limited to, its ability to generate cash from operations in
excess of its fixed charges and other expenses, as well as factors outside of its control, such as the availability of debt and equity
financing on favorable terms. Past performance is not indicative of future results. BTC Yield is a historical looking metric and indicates
no promise, guarantee, recommendation or advice regarding future performance of KULR common stock, operating performance, bitcoin holdings,
or the performance of bitcoin itself.</p>

<P STYLE="font: 12pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: center"><B>KULR Expands Bitcoin Holdings to 800 BTC, Reports
220.2% BTC Yield</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><B>HOUSTON / GLOBENEWSWIRE / May 20, 2025 / <U>KULR Technology Group,
Inc.</U></B> (NYSE American: KULR) (the &quot;Company&quot; or &quot;KULR&quot;), a global leader in sustainable energy management, today
announced it has increased its bitcoin acquisitions for its Bitcoin Treasury by an additional $9 million to reach a total of $78 million
in bitcoin acquisitions. The additional bitcoin were acquired at a weighted average price of $103,234 per bitcoin, inclusive of fees and
expenses. The Company now holds 800.3 BTC.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">This strategic move aligns with KULR&rsquo;s Bitcoin Treasury Strategy
announced on December 4, 2024, wherein the Company committed up to 90% of its surplus cash reserves to be held in bitcoin.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><B>BTC Yield as a Key Performance Indicator</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">Year to date, KULR has achieved a BTC Yield of 220.2%, leveraging a
combination of surplus cash and its At-The-Market (ATM) equity program to fund acquisitions.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">KULR uses &quot;BTC Yield&quot; as a key performance indicator (KPI)
for its Bitcoin Treasury strategy. BTC Yield is calculated as the percentage change period-to-period in the ratio of the Company&rsquo;s
bitcoin holdings to its Assumed Fully Diluted Shares Outstanding. This KPI helps assess the effectiveness of KULR&rsquo;s bitcoin acquisition
strategy in a manner KULR believes drives shareholder value.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><B>Important Considerations Regarding BTC Yield</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">BTC Yield is intended to provide insights into KULR&rsquo;s bitcoin
acquisition strategy but should not be interpreted as a measure of operating performance, financial return, or liquidity. It is not equivalent
to traditional yield metrics, nor does it account for the Company&rsquo;s liabilities or broader financial position.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">The trading price of KULR&rsquo;s common stock is influenced by multiple
factors beyond bitcoin holdings, and BTC Yield does not predict or reflect the stock's market value. Investors should consider this metric
as a supplementary tool and refer to the Company&rsquo;s financial statements and SEC filings for additional information about the Company&rsquo;s
financial position.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">KULR remains committed to its strategic goals of advancing shareholder
value while adhering to disciplined financial management.</P>
