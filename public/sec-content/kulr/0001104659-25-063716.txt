<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><b>Item 3.03 Material Modification to Rights of Security Holders.</b></p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">KULR Technology Group, Inc.,
a Delaware corporation (the &#8220;Company&#8221;), approved a reverse stock split of the Company&#8217;s issued and outstanding shares
of common stock (&#8220;Common Stock&#8221;), at a ratio of 1-for-8 (the &#8220;Reverse Stock Split&#8221;). The Reverse Stock Split was
duly approved by the stockholders of the Company in February 2024.</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify"><b>Reasons for the Reverse Stock Split</b></p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">The noncompulsory decision
to implement the Reverse Stock Split reflects the Company&#8217;s belief that the Reverse Stock Split could optimize market dynamics,
broaden investor appeal and accessibility, and align the conditions of Common Stock with the best interests of its investors. The Company
cannot provide assurance that the actual effects of the Reverse Stock Split will achieve the desired effects or that, if achieved, such
desired effects will be sustained.</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><b>Effects of the Reverse Stock Split</b></p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><i>Effective Date; Symbol; CUSIP Number</i></p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">The Reverse Stock Split became
effective on June&#160;23, 2025 (the &#8220;Effective Date&#8221;). The Common Stock began trading on a split-adjusted basis at the commencement
of trading on June&#160;23, 2025, under the Company&#8217;s existing trading symbol &#8220;KULR.&#8221; The Common Stock was assigned
a new CUSIP number (50125G 307) in connection with the Reverse Stock Split.</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><i>Split Adjustment; Treatment of Fractional Shares</i></p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">On the Effective Date, the
total number of shares of Common Stock held by each stockholder of the Company were exchanged for the number of shares of Common Stock
equal to the number of issued and outstanding shares of Common Stock held by each such stockholder immediately prior to the Reverse Stock
Split, divided by eight (8), with such resulting number of shares rounded up to the nearest whole share. As a result, no fractional shares
were issued in connection with the Reverse Stock Split and no cash or other consideration was paid in connection with any fractional shares
that would otherwise have resulted from the Reverse Stock Split. Also on the Effective Date, all equity awards outstanding immediately
prior to the Reverse Stock Split were adjusted to reflect the Reverse Stock Split.</p>
