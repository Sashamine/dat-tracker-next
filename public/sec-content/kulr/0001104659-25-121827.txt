<P STYLE="font: 14pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: center"><B>KULR Technology Group Enters Joint Development
Collaboration for $100M AI Server Rack Battery Backup Unit Opportunity</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 14pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: center"></P>

<P STYLE="font: 14pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: center">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><B>HOUSTON / GLOBENEWSWIRE / December 17, 2025 / <U>KULR Technology
Group, Inc.</U></B> (NYSE American: KULR) (the &quot;Company&quot; or &quot;KULR&quot;), a <FONT STYLE="color: #212121">Bitcoin+ Treasury
company that builds a portfolio of frontier high-performance energy systems for cross-industry applications,</FONT> today announced it
has entered into a Joint Development Collaboration with a leading global battery-cell manufacturer to co-develop a next-generation KULR
ONE&reg; MAX Battery Backup Unit (BBU) product line designed for AI-scale data centers and high-power compute environments.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">KULR will lead the design, development, safety engineering, and UL
certification of a state-of-the-art BBU architecture built around a new ultra-high-power 21700 cell platform. The collaboration advances
KULR&rsquo;s strategic position in the AI data-center energy market, establishing the foundation for a commercial program with up to $100
million in projected value.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="margin: 0pt 0; font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif">&quot;AI-scale computing is fundamentally reshaping how data centers
think about power,&nbsp;safety&nbsp;and reliability,&rdquo;&nbsp;said Michael Mo, Chief Executive Officer of KULR Technology Group. &ldquo;KULR
has spent years building energy systems designed to perform safely and consistently in the most demanding environments, and this initiative
allows us to extend that foundation into next-generation battery backup solutions,&nbsp;purpose-built&nbsp;for modern AI workloads. As
compute density and energy intensity continue to rise, we see a clear opportunity to&nbsp;deliver scalable, certifiable battery infrastructure
that meets where the industry&nbsp;its&nbsp;headed.&quot;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><B>Advanced 21700-Cell Platform for AI Compute, Hyperscale, and Edge
Deployments</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">The jointly developed BBU system will be optimized around an ultra-high-power
21700 cell architecture capable of supporting the next generation of ultra-high-current and high-power AI server workloads.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">The platform is engineered to meet</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<TABLE CELLPADDING="0" CELLSPACING="0" WIDTH="100%" STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt"><TR STYLE="vertical-align: top">
<TD STYLE="width: 0.25in"></TD><TD STYLE="width: 0.25in"><FONT STYLE="font-family: Symbol">&middot;</FONT></TD><TD>Meta Open Compute Project (OCP) ORV3 standard.</TD></TR></TABLE>

<TABLE CELLPADDING="0" CELLSPACING="0" WIDTH="100%" STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt"><TR STYLE="vertical-align: top">
<TD STYLE="width: 0.25in"></TD><TD STYLE="width: 0.25in"><FONT STYLE="font-family: Symbol">&middot;</FONT></TD><TD>NVIDIA GPU roadmap for 800 HVDC requirements.</TD></TR></TABLE>

<P STYLE="margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt; font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">KULR will provide system-level architecture, thermal-management engineering,
propagation-resistance design, and lead all UL 9540 and UL 9540A certification activities. The partner cell manufacturer will support
cell-level integration and provide technical resources, as needed.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><B>Long-Term Supply Commitment Supporting Commercial Scale</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">Upon successful UL 9540 certification, KULR&rsquo;s joint-development
partner has committed to supplying its ultra-high-power 21700 cells for the newly developed BBU product line throughout the lifetime of
the program. This ensures KULR has predictable, scalable, cost-controlled access to premium high-performance cells as it moves into commercial
production.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">This venture accelerates KULR&rsquo;s continued efforts to meet growing
global demand for safe, certifiable, high-power lithium-ion backup systems across global hyperscalers, cloud service providers, AI data-center
operators, and system integrators.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>
