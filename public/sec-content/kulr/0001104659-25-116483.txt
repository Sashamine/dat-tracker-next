<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify; background-color: white"><b>Item 5.07 Submission

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in; background-color: white">On
November 21, 2025, KULR Technology Group, Inc. (&#8220;Company&#8221;) virtually held its Annual General Meeting of Stockholders. A total
of 21,072,843 shares of common stock and 1,000,000 shares of the Series A voting preferred stock, representing in aggregate 120,083,605
or approximately 83.64% of the shares eligible to vote and constituting a quorum, were represented in person or by valid proxies at the
meeting.</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in; background-color: white">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in; background-color: white">The
stockholders approved the election of Michael Mo, Joanna D. Massey, Donna H. Grier, Aron Schwartz, and Shawn Canter to serve as directors
of the Company until the next annual meeting of the stockholders.</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in; background-color: white">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in; background-color: white">The
stockholders ratified the appointment of CBIZ CPAs P.C. as the Company&#8217;s independent registered public accounting firm for the fiscal
year ending December 31, 2025.</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in; background-color: white">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in; background-color: white">The
stockholders approved the KULR Technology Group, Inc., 2025 Equity Incentive Plan.</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in; background-color: white">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in; background-color: white">The
stockholders approved, on an advisory basis, the compensation of the named executive officers.</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify; background-color: white">&#160;</p>
