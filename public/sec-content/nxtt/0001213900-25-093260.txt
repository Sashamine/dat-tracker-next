the continued listing of its common stock was not warranted. The Company timely requested a hearing before a Nasdaq hearing panel, which
automatically stayed any suspension or delisting action pending the hearing.</p>

7, 2025. In advance of the hearing, the Company submitted written materials, held telephonic discussions with Nasdaq and filed a Current
Report on Form 8-K on September 26, 2025, disclosing the Company&#8217;s recent business development.</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">On September 29, 2025, the Company received a
letter from Nasdaq stating that Nasdaq has determined the Company does have an operating business, and is not a &#8220;public shell,&#8221;
and that Nasdaq is withdrawing the delisting determination. Nasdaq indicated that this matter is closed, and the Company&#8217;s common
stock will remain listed on the Nasdaq Capital Market. No further action is required with respect to this matter.</p>
