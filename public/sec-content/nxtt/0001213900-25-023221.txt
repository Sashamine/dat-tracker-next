<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0"><b>Item 1.01 Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement</b></p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify">As previously disclosed in the Current Report on Form
8-K filed by Next Technology Holding Inc. (formerly known as WeTrade Group Inc. (the &#8220;Company&#8221;))&#160;with the Securities
and Exchange Commission on September 27, 2024 (the &#8220;Previous Form 8-K&#8221;), the Company entered into an Amended and Restated
BTC Trading Contract (the &#8220;Amended BTC Contract&#8221;), dated as of September 24, 2024, with an autonomous organization (the &#8220;Association
Seller), which supports its members in the sale of bitcoins (&#8220;BTC&#8221;).</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify">Under the Amended BTC Contract, the Company is entitled
to purchase up to 5,167 BTC (the &#8220;Total BTC&#8221;) from certain members of the Association Seller set forth on Schedule I of the
Amended BTC Contract (the &#8220;BTC Sellers&#8221;) through the Association Seller at a purchase price of US$30,000 per BTC (subject
to an additional purchase price by issuance of warrants to purchase shares of Common Stock at a nominal exercise price as described below)
over a 12-month period ending on September 24, 2025. The purchase price for the Total BTC will be paid by the Company in cash or shares
of common stock of the Company (the &#8220;Common Stock&#8221;) (including equity securities exercisable for the shares of the Common
Stock). Although the Amended BTC Contract states that the Association Seller (Party B) &#8220;owns the virtual currency&#8221;, to our

members of the Association Seller, not the Association Seller itself, own the BTC to be sold under the Amended BTC Contract.</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify">At the time when the Amended BTC Contract was signed,
the Company indicated its intent to exercise the option to purchase 5,000 BTC out of the Total BTC pursuant to the Amended BTC Contract
(the &#8220;Amended 5,000 BTC Transaction&#8221;). According to the terms of the Amended BTC Contract, the previously-made prepayment
amount of $12,125,500 will be applied towards the total purchase price for the Amended 5,000 BTC Transaction and the Company will pay
the remaining balance through (i) the issuance of 135,171,078 shares of Common Stock (the &#8220;Shares&#8221;) valued at $1.02 per share
and (ii) the issuance of warrants to purchase 294,117,647 shares of Common Stock at a nominal exercise price (the &#8220;Warrants&#8221;,
and the shares issuable under the Warrants, the &#8220;Warrant Shares&#8221;). Using the same per share valuation, the Warrants are worth
approximately $300,000,000. The exercise period for each Warrant is five (5) years from the initial exercise of such Warrant.</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify">&#160;</p>

Shares and the Warrants. Concurrently with the issuance of the Warrants, the BTC Sellers indicated to the Company of their intent to immediately
exercise the Warrants to purchase all of the Warrant Shares thereunder. Accordingly, the Company issued to each BTC Seller the respective
