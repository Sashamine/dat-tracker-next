<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify"><b>Item 3.03 Material Modification to Rights of
Security Holders.</b></p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify"><b>&#160;</b></p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">On June 20, 2025, Next Technology Holding Inc.,
a Wyoming corporation (the &#8220;Company&#8221;) held the annual stockholders meeting and approved a proposal to authorize the Company&#8217;s
board of directors (the &#8220;Board&#8221;) to effect one or multiple reverse stock splits (each, a &#8220;Reverse Stock Split&#8221;)
of all outstanding shares of the Company&#8217;s common stock, par value $0.00 per share (&#8220;Common Stock&#8221;), at a ratio ranging
from any whole number between 1-for-10 and 1-for-250, with the actual split ratio and the split effective time to be determined by the
Board in its discretion.</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">On August 28, 2025, the Board approved a Reverse
Stock Split at a ratio of 200-for-1 (the &#8220;Specific Reverse Stock Split&#8221;). The Specific Reverse Stock Split will become effective
at 12:01 a.m., Eastern Time on September 16, 2025 (the &#8220;Effective Time&#8221;). The Company&#8217;s shares of Common Stock will
begin to trade on a split-adjusted basis on the Nasdaq Capital Market at the commencement of trading on September 16, 2025, under the
Company&#8217;s existing trading symbol &#8220;NXTT&#8221;. The Company&#8217;s Common Stock has been assigned a new CUSIP number of 961884301
in connection with the Specific Reverse Stock Split.</p>
