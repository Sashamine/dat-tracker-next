<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt"> <b>Item 1.01</b> <b>Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement</b></p>



<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify; background-color: white">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify; background-color: white">On November 21, 2025, Next
Investment Group Limited (the &#8220;Company Sub&#8221;), a wholly-owned subsidiary of Next Technology Holding Inc. (formerly known as
WeTrade Group Inc.), a Wyoming corporation, signed a non-binding Memorandum of Understanding (the &#8220;MOU&#8221;) with Global Nexgen
Limited (the &#8220;Global Nexgen&#8221;) to explore a potential strategic transaction involving Bitcoin (&#8220;BTC&#8221;).</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify; background-color: white">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify; background-color: white">The MOU states the Company
Sub&#8217;s non-binding intent to pursue one of two potential transactions within a one-year period from the effective date of the MOU,
with the price for Bitcoin fixed at $84,000 per BTC (the &#8220;Locked Price&#8221;).</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify; background-color: white">&#160;</p>

<table cellpadding="0" cellspacing="0" style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; width: 100%; margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 0; background-color: white"><tr style="vertical-align: top">
<td style="width: 18pt"/><td style="width: 22.5pt">1.</td><td style="text-align: justify">BTC Purchase: The Company Sub may buy up to 10,000 BTC directly from Global Nexgen at the Locked Price.</td></tr></table>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0 0 0 40.5pt; text-align: justify; text-indent: -22.5pt; background-color: white">&#160;</p>

<table cellpadding="0" cellspacing="0" style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; width: 100%; margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 0; background-color: white"><tr style="vertical-align: top">
<td style="width: 18pt"/><td style="width: 22.5pt">2.</td><td style="text-align: justify">Acquisition of Global Nexgen: The Company Sub may acquire Global Nexgen, at a total purchase price calculated
by multiplying the total BTC held by Global Nexgen at closing by the Locked Price.</td></tr></table>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify; background-color: white">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify; background-color: white">While the transaction itself
is non-binding, the MOU requires both parties to negotiate exclusively with each other for a 90-day period and to keep the discussions
confidential.</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify; background-color: white">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify; background-color: white">The parties intend to negotiate
a definitive agreement within the 90-day exclusivity period. However, this MOU is only an expression of intent. There can be no assurance
that a final agreement will be reached or that any transaction will be completed.</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify; background-color: white">&#160;</p>

the full text of the MOU, which is filed as Exhibit 10.1 to this Current Report on Form 8-K.</p>
