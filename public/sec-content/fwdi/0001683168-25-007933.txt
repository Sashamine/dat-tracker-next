<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify"><i>Share Repurchase Program</i></p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">On November 3, 2025, the Board of Directors of
Forward Industries, Inc. (the &#8220;Company&#8221;) authorized a stock repurchase program, pursuant to which the Company may repurchase
up to $1 billion of the Company&#8217;s outstanding shares of common stock, exclusive of any fees, commissions and other expenses related
to such repurchases, from time to time. The authorization will expire on September 30, 2027. Shares may be repurchased under the program
through open market purchases, block trades and/or privately negotiated transactions (including accelerated share repurchase transactions),
related derivative transactions or pursuant to a Rule 10b5-1 trading plan.</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">The extent to which the Company repurchases shares
of its common stock, and the timing of such repurchases, will depend upon a variety of factors, including market conditions, regulatory
requirements and other corporate considerations, as determined by the Company&#8217;s management. The stock repurchase program may be
suspended or discontinued at any time.</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">&#160;</p>
