<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify; background-color: white"><B>Item 1.01 - Entry
into Material Agreement</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify"><I>&#160;</I></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify"><I>Antalpha Loan Agreement</I></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">&#160;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">On October 6, 2025, Naka SPV 2, LLC (the
&#8220;Company&#8221;), a Delaware limited liability company and wholly owned subsidiary of Kindly MD, Inc. ( &#8220;Kindly
MD&#8221;), entered into a Master Loan Agreement, including a loan confirmation thereunder, with Antalpha Digital Pte. Ltd., a
private company organized under the laws of Singapore (&#8220;Antalpha&#8221;) (such Master Loan Agreement being referred to as, the
&#8220;Antalpha Loan Agreement&#8221;). The Antalpha Loan Agreement contemplates Antalpha extending a term loan facility in an
aggregate principal amount of 206,000,000 USDT (the &#8220;Antalpha Loan&#8221;), bearing interest at a rate of 7.0% per annum. The
Antalpha Loan will be advanced in separate tranches over a period of up to five days to allow for the transfer of collateral from
Company to Lender. The first tranche of indebtedness will be equal to an aggregate amount of up to 150,000,000 USDT.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">&#160;</P>
