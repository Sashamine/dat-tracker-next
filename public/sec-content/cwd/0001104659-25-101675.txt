<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&#160;</p>

the minimum $2.5 million stockholders&#8217; equity requirement for continued listing. The Company estimates that as of September&#160;30,
2025, stockholders&#8217; equity is between $4.5 million and $6.0 million.</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&#160;</p>
