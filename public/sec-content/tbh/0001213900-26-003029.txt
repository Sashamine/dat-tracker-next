<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify"><b>Item 3.01. Notice of Delisting of Failure to Satisfy
a Continued Listing Rule or Standard; Transfer of Listing.</b></p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">On January 6, 2026, Brag House
Holdings, Inc. (the &#8220;<b>Company</b>&#8221;) received a deficiency letter (the &#8220;<b>Notice</b>&#8221;) from the Listing Qualifications
Department of The Nasdaq Stock Market LLC (&#8220;<b>Nasdaq</b>&#8221;) notifying the Company that, based upon the closing&#160;bid price&#160;of
the Company&#8217;s common stock, par value $0.0001 per share (the &#8220;<b>Common Stock</b>&#8221;), for the last 30 consecutive business
days, the Company is not currently in compliance with the requirement to maintain a minimum&#160;bid price&#160;of $1.00 per share for
continued listing on The Nasdaq Capital Market, as set forth in Nasdaq Listing Rule 5550(a)(2) (the &#8220;<b>Minimum Bid Requirement</b>&#8221;).</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">The Notice has no immediate effect
on the continued listing status of the Common Stock on The Nasdaq Capital Market, and, therefore, the Company&#8217;s listing remains
fully effective.</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">In accordance with Nasdaq Listing
Rule 5810(c)(3)(A), the Company is provided a compliance period of 180 calendar days from the date of the Notice, or until July 6, 2026,
to regain compliance with the Minimum Bid Requirement. To regain compliance, the closing&#160;bid price&#160;of the Common Stock must
meet or exceed $1.00 per share for a minimum of ten consecutive business days prior to July 6, 2026.</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">If the Company is not in compliance
with the Minimum Bid Requirement by July 6, 2026, the Company may be afforded a second 180 calendar day compliance period. To qualify
for this additional compliance period, the Company will be required to meet the continued listing requirement for market value of publicly
held shares and all other initial listing standards for The Nasdaq Capital Market, with the exception of the Minimum&#160;Bid Price&#160;requirement.</p>
