## Company Overview

- **Business**: Casual apparel manufacturing/sales for teens/20s, high e-commerce ratio
- **Current Stock Price**: ¥245 (as of Feb 2, 2026)
- **Market Cap**: ¥9.95 billion (~$66M USD)

## Bitcoin Strategy Timeline

### Strategy Launch: April 2025
ANAP Holdings announced their first Bitcoin purchase on **April 16, 2025**, making them one of Japan's earliest public companies to adopt a corporate Bitcoin treasury strategy (similar to MicroStrategy).
