## Bitcoin Treasury Strategy

Remixpoint began its Bitcoin treasury strategy in **late September 2024**, motivated by:
- Yen depreciation hedge
- Asset diversification
- Bitcoin's post-halving price appreciation
- US spot ETF approval and institutional adoption
- Post-US election crypto market optimism

Their strategy is part of a broader cash management approach to reduce JPY exposure and hedge against currency risk.
