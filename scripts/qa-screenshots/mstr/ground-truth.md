### mNAV Formula

```
CryptoNAV = (BTC Holdings × BTC Price) + cashForCrypto
EV = marketCap + totalDebt + preferredEquity - excessCash
mNAV = EV / CryptoNAV
```

**Note:** Full mNAV calculation requires:
1. Current BTC price
2. Total debt outstanding (from balance sheet)
3. Preferred stock liquidation value
4. Market cap (price × shares)
