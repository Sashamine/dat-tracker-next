### Why This Matters

The "Diluted weighted average" is a mathematical construct for EPS calculations that:
1. Weights shares by the time period they were outstanding during the quarter
2. Includes dilutive securities (options, warrants) hypothetically exercised
3. Is NOT the actual count of shares that exist

The **cover page share count** is the official, audited number of shares actually outstanding at a specific date.
