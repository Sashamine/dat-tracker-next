## Other Notable Q4 2024 Events

### $21 Billion ATM Sales Agreement
- **October 30, 2024:** New Sales Agreement with TD Securities, Barclays, Benchmark, BTIG, Canaccord, Cantor Fitzgerald, Maxim, Mizuho, and SG Americas
- Allows issuance of up to $21 billion in class A common stock
- File: `8k-2024-10-30-247543.html`

### 2029 Convertible Notes Offering
- **November 21, 2024:** Completed $3.0 billion offering of 0% convertible senior notes due 2029
- Conversion premium: ~55%
- Net proceeds: ~$2.97 billion
- File: `8k-2024-11-25-264733.html`

### Loss of "Controlled Company" Status
- **~November 12, 2024:** Michael Saylor's voting power dropped below 50%
- Company no longer qualifies for "controlled company" exemption under Nasdaq rules
- Nominating Committee established
- File: `8k-2024-11-12-255184.html`

### Nasdaq 100 Inclusion
- **December 23, 2024:** MSTR added to Nasdaq 100 Index
- Effective before market open on December 23, 2024

### ASU 2023-08 Adoption (Crypto Accounting)
- **Effective January 1, 2025:** Adopted new accounting standard for crypto assets
- Bitcoin now measured at fair value (mark-to-market)
- Cumulative-effect adjustment: $12.745 billion increase to retained earnings
